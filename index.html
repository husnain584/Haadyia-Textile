Manufacturing

Weaving

We have 100 Auto looms and 20 Air jet looms upto 130” widths. Our weaving department is all geared up to face the challenges of the textile industry. The weaving facilities are capable of producing twills, drills, dobbies, satins, sheeting, and other flawless finely woven fabrics.

Processing/Fabric Dyeing

Jigger dyeing, ranging from 72” to 120” in width, to be dyed in roll form. Soft flow & Pandora for dyeing soft & delicate fabrics in rope form. Drying ranges consist of vertical drying ranges, close chamber Stenter or tumble dryer to suit each quality of fabrics.

Cutting/Stitching Operation

Our cut-sew operations are capable of producing approximately thousand of pieces annually of made-up articles, comprising Bed sheets, Duvet covers, bedspreads,, pillow case/Tick, cushions and Comforter etc We have facility of Single needle, Zig Zag stitching, Over-locking etc.

Checking & Packing Area

Without a proper check and balance, it’s nearly impossible to deliver excellence. Since we are strong believers in delivering reliable and quality products, we have one of the best textile laboratories equipped with the latest technology.

Finished Goods warehouses

We operate modern warehouses with added benefits. Working with you to develop a tailor-made concept to meet your needs and demands. We set our focus on optimization and cost effectiveness. Our customer-oriented supply chain has a significant advantage over the competition.
